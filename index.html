Understanding the Armed Forces Compensation Scheme

The Armed Forces Compensation Scheme is a crucial support system for UK service personnel and veterans who have suffered injuries or illness due to their service. Established in 2005, the AFCS provides compensation and support to eligible individuals, ensuring that those who have sacrificed for our nation receive the care and assistance they deserve.
